How It Works

Join the thrifting revolution in 5 simple, transparent steps.

Step 1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email or social credentials. It takes less than a minute to join the circular fashion network.

Step 2

Build Your Seller Profile

Add a custom username, display avatar, store bio, and link your city location to establish credit.

Step 3

Upload Product Photos

Snap clear photos of garments, specify original vs. thrift pricing, size details, and brand name tags.

Step 4

Connect with Buyers

Negotiate securely via comments or messages, answer sizing questions, and arrange transit details.

Step 5

Sell and Earn

Pack the garment safely, ship it using our delivery partners, and receive payments straight to your bank account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about buying, selling, and security policies on Closeit.in.

Simply click on the product you like, check the seller's bio/ratings, and press the "Message" button or purchase button to secure the item. We host secure options to complete your transactions.

Once you sign up, complete your seller profile settings. Tap "Upload Listing", attach 3-4 clear photos, state sizing details, brand names, location, and the thrift price. Your listing goes live instantly!

Listing products is completely free. We charge a small nominal transaction platform fee of 8% only when your item successfully sells to support secure processing and platform maintenance.

Sellers are responsible for packing and shipping the item within 3 days of booking. We offer automated shipping integrations where a courier pickup can be scheduled directly from your address.

Since thrift listings are peer-to-peer pre-owned clothes, standard returns are not supported unless the seller states otherwise or the item is significantly misdescribed. Ask the seller for precise dimensions before buying.

Yes. Our escrow system holds payment securely and only releases funds to the seller once the tracking indicates that the package has been delivered and checked by the buyer.

Sellers are strictly instructed to list only authentic items. Buyers can request invoice receipts or check neck tag details. Additionally, we moderate listings and ban accounts violating authenticity guidelines.

We accept all digital payment methods in India, including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit/Credit Cards, and Net Banking options via our checkout partner.

Orders can only be canceled if the seller has not dispatched the package. Contact the seller immediately through the in-app chat to request cancellation.

After a transaction concludes successfully, the buyer is prompted to leave a rating (1-5 stars) and review comment. High ratings help sellers gain Trust Badges and increase future sales.

Yes. Listed clothes must be clean and in wearable shape. Innerwear, copy counterfeits without tags, weapons, or items unrelated to fashion are strictly banned from our marketplace database.

Yes, peer-to-peer thrifting thrives on bargains! Tap the "Message" button on the item card to pitch an offer. Once both agree, the price can be updated for purchase.

India is a massive global textile hub. Embracing thrift fashion helps reduce dye chemical runoffs, limits carbon footprints, and diverts usable fabrics from landing inside local landfills.

If tracking stalls, report the issue to Closeit.in Support. We will coordinate with the logistics partner. If verified lost, the buyer gets a full refund, and the seller receives shipping insurance cover.

You can go to Account Settings > Delete Account. Make sure all pending payments are cleared and all active buyer orders are fulfilled before deleting your database profile.
