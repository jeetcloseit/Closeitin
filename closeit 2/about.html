Sustainable Fashion For All

Closeit.in is India's premier community-driven digital bazaar connecting conscious consumers with curators of pre-loved apparel.

Our Story: The Birth of Closeit.in

The story of Closeit.in began in early 2024, sparked by a simple observation of changing youth cultures across metropolitan Indian cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore. Our founders, a team of young fashion enthusiasts and tech developers, noticed that the wardrobes of Gen Z and millennials were spinning in a cycle of rapid fast-fashion acquisition. Social media cycles demanded new trends every week, leading to piles of cheap, underutilized garments that ended up either in landfills or forgotten at the backs of closets. At the same time, a growing community of vintage curators, thrift collectors, and circular fashion advocates was springing up on social channels, primarily Instagram. However, these independent creators faced massive hurdles: chaotic direct message negotiations, lack of payment security, zero shipping automation, and the inability to build discoverable storefronts outside Instagram's changing algorithm.

Recognizing this vacuum, we set out to build Closeit.in. We wanted to design a bridge that combined the highly visual, interactive, profile-centric feel of Instagram with the structured trust, secure escrow payment pipelines, and shipping ease of modern e-commerce platforms. Closeit.in was founded to democratize thrift fashion in India, making it as effortless and premium to buy a pre-owned jacket or sneaker as it is to purchase a brand-new item. We spent months talking to thrift sellers, understanding their operational paint points, and listening to buyers who were skeptical about second-hand quality. By offering verified profile systems, star ratings, automated delivery pickups, and a protected transaction escrow system, we turned a fragmented black-market thrift ecosystem into a structured, credible community of fashion lovers.

"Thrifting is not just about finding low-cost branded clothes; it is an active statement against the carbon-intensive manufacturing practices of modern fast fashion."

Today, Closeit.in has grown from a small prototype into a flourishing nationwide network connecting over 10,000 verified sellers with more than 50,000 listings. From college students selling outfits they've outgrown to professional vintage curators importing rare 90s streetwear garments, our platform hosts a highly diverse fashion catalog. Our office in Gurgaon represents our commitment to creating sustainable tech infrastructure that supports the transition to a circular green economy in India. We believe every piece of clothing deserves a second life, and every seller deserves a stage to showcase their curation talent.

Mission & Vision: Shaping the Future of Retail

Our Mission: To accelerate India's transition toward a circular economy by establishing pre-loved fashion as a mainstream, aspirational, and secure alternative to linear fast-fashion. We aim to empower millions of micro-entrepreneurs to monetize their wardrobes while providing buyers with authentic, high-quality, and highly affordable sustainable apparel.

Our Vision: To become India's primary destination for fashion discovery and social commerce. We envision a future where 50% of the apparel consumed in Indian urban centers has been recirculated, where the stigma associated with second-hand goods is replaced by pride in sustainable curation, and where Closeit.in serves as the technological engine powering this lifestyle evolution.

To realize this vision, our engineering and product design teams work on making thrift shopping interactive and fun. We are continuously refining the social features of Closeit.in. We want our users to interact like they are on a social network—following their favorite curators, commenting on outfits, liking new drop notifications, and sharing styled collections—while retaining the security guarantees of institutional e-commerce. By reducing the friction involved in listing items and eliminating the risk of payment scams, we are unlocking a hidden supply of garments residing in millions of Indian households.

Sustainability & The Circular Fashion Model

The global fashion industry is responsible for nearly 10% of global carbon emissions, which is more than all international flights and maritime shipping combined. The rise of synthetic fabrics like polyester has turned clothes into cheap plastic disposables that shed microplastics into water bodies and take hundreds of years to decompose. In India, the environmental impact is particularly severe. As one of the world's largest hubs for textile manufacturing and garment assembly, our rivers and agricultural lands bear the toxic brunt of chemical dyes and high water consumption. Manufacturing a single pair of new denim jeans requires nearly 7,500 liters of water—an amount an average human drinks over seven years.

Closeit.in exists to interrupt this resource-intensive cycle. Every transaction on our platform represents an item of clothing that does not need to be manufactured from scratch, saving thousands of liters of clean water, preventing pesticide use in cotton fields, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. In 2025 alone, our community's trading activity prevented an estimated 120 metric tons of CO2 emissions from entering the atmosphere and saved millions of liters of water. This is the power of the circular economy: extending the lifespan of a garment by just nine months reduces its carbon, water, and waste footprints by approximately 20% to 30%.

Additionally, we encourage our sellers to use eco-friendly, biodegradable packaging, such as cardboard boxes and paper mailers, rather than single-use plastic wraps. We believe that sustainability should extend to the entire packaging and delivery lifecycle. By collaborating with local courier companies that use electric vehicle fleets for urban deliveries, we strive to reduce the carbon footprint of transport. Circular fashion is not a passing trend; it is an absolute environmental necessity. Closeit.in is proud to lead this charge in India, proving that style and ecological responsibility can coexist beautifully.

Community Building: Trust & Safety First

The foundation of any successful marketplace is trust. Unlike traditional business-to-consumer platforms where brands sell directly to customers, Closeit.in is a peer-to-peer network. This means the seller on the other side could be a student in Delhi, a creative artist in Bangalore, or a vintage store owner in Kochi. To ensure a safe, friendly, and reliable environment for everyone, we have established robust community guidelines and verification pipelines.

Every seller profile on our platform undergoes a verification check before they are allowed to list luxury or high-demand streetwear brands. We look for active social media presence, verified contact details, and initial rating benchmarks. Our review system allows buyers to transparently rate their purchasing experience, which forms a permanent part of the seller's profile page. This self-regulating mechanism incentivizes sellers to accurately describe garment conditions, highlight any minor defects, wash the items thoroughly before dispatch, and package them with care.

Furthermore, our escrow payment system ensures that a buyer's money remains safe. When you buy an item on Closeit.in, the payment is held in a secure trust account. The seller is notified to ship the item, and the funds are released only when the tracking system confirms delivery and the buyer has had a 48-hour window to flag any authenticity or damage issues. We also maintain a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods. Sellers found listing fake items with intention are immediately suspended, protecting the integrity of our marketplace and preserving the premium experience of our users.

Future Plans: Expanding the Circular Frontier

While our digital platform has achieved significant scale, we are just scratch-coating the surface of what is possible for sustainable fashion in India. Over the next five years, Closeit.in plans to expand both horizontally and vertically to deepen our environmental impact and enrich our user experience.

  • Physical Pop-up Markets & Fleas: We plan to bring Closeit.in to life offline by hosting monthly thrift flea markets in tier-1 and tier-2 Indian cities, allowing buyers to meet curators, inspect clothes, and experience the community vibe physically.
  • AI Sizing & Fit Matching: To resolve the sizing challenges of vintage and pre-loved clothing, we are developing AI-powered styling recommendations that compare measurements against standard brands to suggest the perfect fit.
  • Closeit Authentication Hubs: For premium streetwear, designer labels, and rare collectible sneakers, we will introduce a middleman authentication service. Sellers can ship premium items to our hubs, where experts verify authenticity and condition before routing them to buyers.
  • Brand Recycling Collaborations: We aim to partner with mainstream Indian fashion brands, allowing customers to trade in their used garments for store credits while Closeit.in handles the resale, recycling, or upcycling.

At Closeit.in, we believe that the future of fashion is pre-loved. We invite you to join us on this journey—whether you are listing your first pre-loved tee, hunting for a rare vintage sweatshirt, or simply subscribing to our newsletter. Together, we can rewrite India's fashion narrative, one garment at a time, and build a stylish, sustainable tomorrow.
