Quick Contact Help

Before reaching out, check if these quick support answers help solve your query.

Where is my package?

You can track shipping status via the tracker code sent to your registered email and profile dashboard after order dispatch.

How do I request a refund?

If an item is damaged or counterfeit, log in to your profile, go to Purchases, and tap "Raise Dispute" within 48 hours of delivery.

How to edit my listing?

Navigate to your Store Listings tab, select the item card you wish to modify, and tap "Edit details" to change pricing or description.
